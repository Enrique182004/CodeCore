What is FirstHack?

FirstHack 2026 is CodeCore's first interdisciplinary hackathon. Five industry partners each bring a real-world problem, and teams of students compete to build the best solution over one unforgettable weekend.

You don't need to be a CS major to join — we encourage every team to reserve at least one seat for a student outside their primary discipline. Diverse perspectives lead to stronger solutions.

Teams of 4–5 students work on the challenge of their choice, guided by workshops and experts, and present their work to company judges for a shot at the Grand Showcase.

Two Tracks, One Goal

Every challenge runs across both tracks. Pick the one that matches your skills — prizes go to the entire team.

How It Works

From meeting the companies to presenting at the Grand Showcase — here's the full competition flow.

1

Challenge Showcase

Companies present their challenges and answer your questions.

2

Workshops

Teams develop their ideas with guidance from experts.

3

Hackathon

Teams build their solutions over one weekend.

4

Company Judging

Each company selects one finalist per track.

5

Grand Showcase

Finalists present their work. Winners are chosen!
