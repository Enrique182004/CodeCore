Our Mission

The mission of CodeCore is to empower UTEP Computer Science students by strengthening their foundation in computer science through peer-led review sessions, collaborative learning, and applied problem-solving. CodeCore is dedicated to creating a supportive community where students grow in confidence, sharpen their coding skills, and prepare for both academic and professional success.

Our Vision

The vision of CodeCore is to cultivate a thriving culture of mentorship, leadership, and collaboration within the Computer Science student body. By fostering strong fundamentals and encouraging knowledge-sharing, CodeCore aims to expand into a hub that not only supports CS students today but also, in the future, unites students across diverse majors through the common language of computer science.

What is CodeCore?

CodeCore is a recognized student organization at The University of Texas at El Paso, founded in Fall 2025. We are a community of Computer Science students dedicated to helping each other succeed through peer-led learning, collaborative problem-solving, and comprehensive review sessions.

Open to all currently enrolled UTEP CS students regardless of classification, CodeCore offers a welcoming environment where everyone can strengthen their understanding of computer science fundamentals and advanced topics.
